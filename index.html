About Thom Yorke:

  • Born 7 October 1968
  • English musician and the main vocalist and songwriter of the rock band Radiohead
  • A multi-instrumentalist, he mainly plays guitar and keyboards and is noted for his falsetto
  • Considered one of the most infuential musicians of his generation

  • People say Radiohead is depressing- but actually, I always think it's kind of hopeful
